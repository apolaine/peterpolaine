---
title: "Paintings"
description: "Oil paintings of landscape, still life, and the figure. Peter's paintings sought to capture the essence of a scene — more time looking than painting, keeping only what first compelled him to paint the subject."
quote: "I have found over a period of time when painting that the first stages had been nearer to what I wanted to achieve, which is of course a moving target. The result is capturing the essence, the feeling, the layout, the colours, balance and design and leaving everything out that would confuse this. Therefore, more time looking than painting and avoiding overworking a painting, which then loses these qualities."
